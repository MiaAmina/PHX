The "Impersonation" Test (The Shadow Button)This is for when a Broker calls you saying, "I can't see the 'Buy Now' button!"The Action: In the Admin User List, find a specific Agent (Retailer). Click the "Login As" or "View as User" button.What to look for: Does your screen change to the Agent Portal view?The Goal: You should see the "Voucher Center" and "Search & Filter" just like they do.Critical Safety Check: When you are "Impersonating," can you still see a button to "Return to Admin Mode"? If you get stuck as the Agent and have to log out/in again, that's a UI bug.4.
