\section*{Slide 2: The Problem --- The ``WhatsApp Chaos''}

Every year, millions of hotel room-nights in Makkah and Madinah are traded through fragmented, manual channels. The result is a broken supply chain.

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{|>{\raggedright\arraybackslash}p{3.5cm}|>{\raggedright\arraybackslash}p{10.5cm}|}
\hline
\rowcolor{headergray}
\textbf{Pain Point} & \textbf{Impact} \\
\hline
\textbf{Opaque Pricing} & Wholesale inventory is negotiated via WhatsApp groups and phone calls with zero price transparency \\
\hline
\textbf{Trust Deficit} & No secure escrow mechanism for massive B2B room-block payments --- disputes are resolved by reputation alone \\
\hline
\textbf{Inventory Deadlock} & Large blocks sit unsold with major brokers while thousands of small travel agents lack access to quality inventory \\
\hline
\textbf{Compliance Gap} & Manual VAT invoicing, no audit trail, and zero integration with ZATCA or Ministry of Hajj systems \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{quote}
``An estimated 60--70\% of seasonal room inventory in Makkah and Madinah is traded with zero digital oversight --- no escrow, no audit trail, no recourse.''
\end{quote}

\section*{Slide 3: The Solution --- The ``Live Pit''}

PHX Exchange is a real-time B2B2C marketplace that connects the three pillars of the Hajj \& Umrah accommodation supply chain:

\vspace{0.5cm}

\subsection*{For Hotels: ``Maximize Your Yield''}
\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item Auction room blocks to a verified broker network
\item Real-time competitive bidding drives prices to true market value
\item Anti-sniping protection ensures fair auction dynamics
\item Ministry BRN assignment for full regulatory compliance
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{For Brokers: ``Trade with Speed''}
\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item Access a transparent secondary market for room inventory
\item Win auctions, apply markup pricing, and distribute to agent networks
\item Private and direct offer channels for whitelisted agents
\item 7-day inventory discipline --- use it or lose it
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{For Agents: ``Book with Certainty''}
\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item Browse a verified marketplace of available rooms
\item Atomic booking with instant escrow protection
\item Register pilgrims, generate PDF vouchers with QR codes
\item ZATCA-compliant tax invoices downloadable on demand
\end{itemize}
