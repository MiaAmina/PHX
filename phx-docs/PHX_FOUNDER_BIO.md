## The Vision Behind PHX Exchange

Amina founded PHX Exchange with a clear conviction: the pilgrimage accommodation market — serving over 10 million pilgrims annually — deserves the same level of technological sophistication, financial protection, and operational transparency that other billion-dollar industries take for granted.

Having witnessed firsthand the inefficiencies, trust issues, and lack of accountability in the Hajj and Umrah accommodation supply chain, Amina set out to build a platform that doesn't simply digitize a broken process, but reimagines it entirely. PHX Exchange introduces open auctions for price transparency, escrow-based payments for financial protection, real-time inventory management to eliminate double-bookings, and built-in regulatory compliance — all in a single, integrated marketplace.

## A Mission-Driven Approach

PHX Exchange is more than a technology platform — it is a tool that empowers the Ministry of Hajj and Umrah with real-time visibility into the accommodation supply chain. Every hotel listing, every broker transaction, every agent booking, and every pilgrim's visa status flows through a single auditable system. The Ministry gains what it has never had before: live data on room allocation, pricing transparency across the entire chain, escrow-protected payments that eliminate fraud, and a direct digital pipeline from pilgrim booking to visa issuance. For the first time, the Ministry can see exactly where pilgrims are housed, what they paid, and whether accommodations meet the standards promised — all without relying on manual reporting from intermediaries.
